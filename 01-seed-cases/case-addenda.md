# Case Addenda — Inquiry Categories & Fake Inquirer Contacts

Two things every seed case needs that were centralized here for efficiency (Sonnet folds these into each
`*.answer-key.json` during S1, and the intake UI in S2 reads them). All contacts are **fictional**.

## Inquiry Category dropdown
Trainees select the inquiry's category at intake, to build the habit of categorizing inquiries. Options:
**Indication · Efficacy · Safety · Pharmacokinetics · Drug-Interactions · Other.**
The "correct" category below is what the evaluator credits (S4.12 — case question categories appropriately
selected). Where an AE/PC/special situation is embedded, the *surface inquiry* sets the category; the safety
signal is captured in the Safety tab, not by re-categorizing the whole inquiry.

| Case | Correct category | Note |
|---|---|---|
| SC-01 | Drug-Interactions | which meds elevate INR |
| SC-02 | Efficacy | "why isn't it working right away" |
| SC-03 | Other | inhaler technique / mouth-rinse (administration) |
| SC-04 | Other | product complaint / replacement (safety captured in Safety tab) |
| SC-05 | Safety | serious rash |
| SC-06 | Efficacy | off-label efficacy data request (still categorized by the ask) |
| SC-07 | Safety | boxed-warning cancer risk question |
| SC-08 | Other | duration of use (administration) |
| SC-09 | Pharmacokinetics | renal dose adjustment |
| SC-10 | Safety | use in pregnancy |
| SC-11 | Other | disposal of unused lower-dose supply (LOE captured in Safety tab) |
| SC-12 | Drug-Interactions | interaction question (hospitalization clarified into Safety tab) |
