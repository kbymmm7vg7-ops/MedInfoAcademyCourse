# Simulation SOP — MedInfo Academy (Fictional, v1.0)

The single SOP world all seed cases and the scorecard reference. Everything here is fictional and
labeled SIMULATION; it stands in for the "client-specific MCMP" of a real engagement.

## Safety reporting
- **Four-element test** (all four required for a valid AE report): (1) identifiable patient, (2) identifiable reporter, (3) suspect product, (4) adverse event.
- **AE reports**: complete Safety tab + route to **Pharmacovigilance (PV)** within **1 business day** of receipt.
- **PC reports**: complete Safety tab + route to **Quality**, within **1 business day**. Capture lot number, expiration date, NDC, availability of suspect product; offer retrieval kit when product is available.
- **Dual AE + PC**: route to **both** PV and Quality (dual routing), one case record.
- **Pregnancy/lactation exposure**: special situation. Flag it, complete AE workflow (exposure is reportable even without an adverse outcome), and refer the reporter to the product's **Pregnancy Exposure Registry** where one exists.
- Other special situations: overdose, misuse/abuse, medication error, lack of effect, product tampering.

## Compliance
- Respond only from PI/USPI, approved SRLs, and approved resources. **No medical advice**; consumers directed to their HCP for treatment decisions.
- **Off-label**: never volunteer. Unsolicited requests from HCPs: acknowledge, state the indication is not approved, offer to process through the standard MI response (written response with balanced data) — never answer promotionally or from memory on a live call.
- Internal sales/commercial personnel asking off-label questions: do not answer; direct them to Medical Affairs through their internal process; document.

## Escalation routes
- Hostile caller / threat of legal action → de-escalate, flag **special situation: legal**, escalate to **supervisor + Legal**; do not speculate or admit fault.
- Media/journalist → do not comment beyond approved public information; capture contact details; route to **Corporate Communications**; flag special situation: media.
- Attorney → collect contact info, no product discussion, route to **Legal**.

## Documentation minimums (mirrors Scorecard S4.3)
- AE/PC and HCP MI contacts: name, background, phone, full address.
- Patients/consumers: name, background, phone, postal code (or city + state).
- Every case: correct product, requester type, solicited/unsolicited, source event, question categories, response route, sources cited in response field.
